Hierologia

An open academic API for the comparative study of sacred traditions. Every entry is sourced, neutral, and versioned.

    A graph of belief, preserved and cited.

    Hierologia keeps theological, historical, and sociological knowledge across religious traditions as structured, neutral, sourced data. The first tradition is Islamic theology (kalam). Each concept, figure, and school links to the others, and every claim carries its sources.

    Choose an entry to begin. Each one is also a live JSON endpoint.
